At Hunter Dunning We Believe In Doing a Job Properly

Combining advanced online search with traditional search and headhunting methods we are building an enviable reputation for sourcing highly qualified skilled professionals within our specialist markets. We achieve our high standards by ensuring that all our consultants and resourcers are well trained industry specialists and can clearly grasp your technical and business issues.

We can provide candidates for permanent and contract work and in all cases we screen applicants before they are put forward to ensure that they are appropriate for the requirement.

With extensive research into the background and experience of our candidates through thorough consultation, we can ensure that the CV's you receive will be accurately selected to complement your company's requirements and ethos.
